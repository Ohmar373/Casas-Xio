Welcome to Casas Xio


At Casas Xio, we believe that your home is a reflection of your dreams and aspirations. Located in the heart of the RGV, we specialize in crafting beautiful, durable homes in the Brownsville area and beyond. Our commitment to quality materials and exceptional craftsmanship ensures that each project is built to last. We understand that building a home is one of life’s most significant journeys. That’s why our dedicated team works closely with you at every step, listening to your vision and turning it into reality.

Whether it’s a cozy family retreat or a modern masterpiece, we’re here to make your dream home come true. Join us in building not just houses, but lasting memories in the vibrant community of South Texas.
